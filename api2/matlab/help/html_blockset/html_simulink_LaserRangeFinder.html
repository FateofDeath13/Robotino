LaserRangeFinder

Robotino can be equipped with a very precise Hokuyo URG-04LX-UG01 laser scanner and distance readings from the laser range finder can be read.

Contents

  • Block properties
  • Examples

Block properties

Inputs

  1. ComId

Outputs

  1. seq - The sequence number.
  2. stamp - Time stamp.
  3. angle_min - Minimum angle in radians.
  4. angle_max - Maximum angle in radians.
  5. angle_increment - Angle between two scans in radians.
  6. time_increment - Time between two scans in seconds.
  7. scan_time - Time to scan the field of view in seconds.
  8. range_min - Minimum range in meters.
  9. range_max - Maximum range in meters.
  10. ranges - The range measurements in meters.
  11. numRanges - Number of range measurements.
  12. intensities - The intensity measurements.
  13. numIntensities - Number of intensity measurements.
